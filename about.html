Hello, I'm Sarah

I started my coding journey when I was 13 years old. It all began when I stumbled upon HTML and CSS while playing a video game called Neopets. Since then, I have attended several coding workshops, a UX/UI bootcamp, and completed a course on accessibility design to cater to a diverse audience.

Before I got into coding and design, I spent five years as a Bioenvironmental Engineer in the US Air Force. This experience allowed me to tackle environmental and occupational health challenges, gave me insight into various work environments, and helped me understand complex systems. It also developed my interest in understanding how people perform tasks and the problems they face.

When I'm not coding, you'll usually find me practicing yoga to find calmness, cooking to satisfy my curiosity, or painting to express my creativity. I'm always eager to learn and grow, both personally and professionally.
