Learn more about me

Hey there! I'm a coder and designer based in Dayton, and I love creating beautiful and accessible designs. It all started for me when I was a teenager and discovered my passion for coding. While serving in the United States Air Force, I had the opportunity to tackle environmental and occupational health challenges. I've also participated in women-focused coding workshops, a UX/UI bootcamp, and accessibility design courses, which have all helped me grow as a coder and designer. When I'm not coding, you'll usually find me practicing yoga to find calmness, cooking to satisfy my curiosity, or painting to express my creativity. I'm always eager to learn and grow, both personally and professionally.
