Learn more about me

I'm a Dayton-based coder and designer passionate about creating beautiful, and accessible designs. During my teenage years, I started my coding journey while playing a video game called Neopets. After serving in the United States Air Force addressing environmental and occupational health challenges, I dove into tech. My experience includes women-focused coding workshops, a UX/UI boot camp, and an accessibility design certification from Udemy.

Beyond coding, I dedicate time to yoga and cooking, which are essential for my well-being and creativity. Yoga keeps me flexible and centered, while cooking allows me to explore new flavors and share joy with my family and friends. Whether it's mastering a challenging yoga pose or experimenting with a new recipe, I thrive on learning and growing, both personally and professionally.
